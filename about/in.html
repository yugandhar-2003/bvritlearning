Bvrit Speciality

  • B V Raju Institute of Technology (BVRIT) was founded in 1997 by the visionary philanthropist, the late Padmabhushan Dr. B.V. Raju, under the Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES).
  • In 2014, BVRIT achieved UGC Autonomous Status.
  • The institute is approved by AICTE, the Government of Telangana, and is affiliated with JNTU, Hyderabad.
  • Our CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, Chemical, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering programs are accredited by NBA, and BVRIT proudly holds an A+ grade accreditation from NAAC.
  • Our institution benefits from the guidance of distinguished professionals from IITs and various engineering and government sectors, led by Sri K. V. Vishnu Raju.
  • He holds a degree in Chemical Engineering from REC, Trichy, and a postgraduate degree from Michigan Technological University, USA.
  • BVRIT was established to provide quality technical education in an environment that fosters the all-round development of students.
  • Under the leadership of our Chairman, Sri K. V. Vishnu Raju, BVRIT has gained a reputation for its exceptional teaching-learning practices, high-quality student intake, excellent infrastructure, and outstanding placement records.
